Our Story

Dilip Fancy Store

Dilip Fancy Store is a trusted name in Jaipur's vibrant Nehru Bazar, offering a wide range of clothing and fancy goods to shoppers from across the city and beyond. Located in the bustling Radio Market area of Pink City, we have built a reputation for stocking quality products at honest, fixed prices — no bargaining needed, because we believe great value speaks for itself.

Our store is staffed by a friendly and knowledgeable team dedicated to helping every customer find exactly what they need. Whether you are looking for everyday wear, festive clothing, or unique fancy accessories, Dilip Fancy Store is your one-stop destination. We are open six days a week from 11:00 AM to 8:00 PM and on Sundays from 12:00 PM, making it easy for you to shop at your convenience.

  • Fixed & transparent pricing — no bargaining, just honest value
  • Highly rated at 4.4/5 stars for quality products and excellent service
  • Conveniently located in Jaipur's famous Nehru Bazar, Pink City
